Rules

Clear the board by removing numberpairs.

Numbers can be removed when

  • They are equal (5 & 5 or 2 & 2)
  • They add up to 10 (3 & 7 or 1 & 9)
  • Are in the same column without other numbers between
  • Horizontaly follow each other without other numbers between
  • The end of a horizontal line connects with the start of the next line

Use the FILL command to add new numbers when you run out of Combinations
