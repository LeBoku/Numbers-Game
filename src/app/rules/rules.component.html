Rules

Your goal is to clear the board by removing numberpairs.

Numbers can be removed if

  • They are equal (e.g 5 & 5)
  • They add up to 10 (e.g 3 & 7 or 1 & 9)
  • Are in the same column without other numbers between
  • Horizontaly follow each other without other numbers between
  • The end of a horizontal line connects with the start of the next line
